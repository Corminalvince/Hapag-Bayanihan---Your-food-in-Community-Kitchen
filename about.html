About Hapag Bayanihan

Building stronger communities through shared meals and mutual support across the Philippines.

Our Mission

Hapag Bayanihan is dedicated to combating food insecurity by connecting community members through a network of shared resources, volunteer drivers, and local kitchens. We believe that no one should go hungry when communities come together.

Our Values

Bayanihan Spirit

Compassion

Dignity

Ensuring every person receives help with respect and without judgment.

Sustainability

Building long-term solutions that strengthen community resilience.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Hapag Bayanihan emerged from the recognition that food insecurity affects millions of Filipinos, yet communities possess the resources and willingness to help each other. By leveraging technology and the inherent spirit of bayanihan, we've created a platform that makes it easier for people to share meals and support their neighbors.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a network spanning multiple neighborhoods, connecting generous community kitchens with families in need, and enabling volunteers to make a direct impact in their communities.
